About me

I dedicate myself to each session in order to tailor to the needs of my client as well as motivating them. To leave their exercise comfort zone in order to achieve optimal results.

As an adolescent and adult I struggled with my weight and overcame personal obstacles which helped me realize the importance of exercise and healthy eating. I personally weighed 300 pounds and was diagnosed with type two diabetes. I lost 150 pounds and today I enjoy running, tennis, cycling, and going to the park with my grand kids.

I have a great passion for fitness and am amazed at the tremendous benefits it provides every person, no matter your age, gender, or physical limitations. My job is to find what works best for each individual and inspire that person to live their best life possible.
